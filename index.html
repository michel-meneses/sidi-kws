Lack of data for KWS? No more!

Keyword spotting (KWS) has become a hot topic in speech processing due to the rise of commercial applications based on voice command detection, such as voice assistants. SiDi KWS intends to boost the development of new KWS systems by provinding the largest multilingual dataset of spoken keywords. It is based on the automatic forced alignment of public datasets of transcribed speech.
